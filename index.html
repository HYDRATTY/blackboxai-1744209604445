Who We Are

HYDRATECH is a dynamic web development and IT support company based in Kenya, dedicated to helping businesses and organizations thrive through tailored digital solutions. From small startups to larger institutions, we design, build, and maintain systems that empower our clients to focus on what they do best — while we handle the tech.

Our team brings together experienced developers, network specialists, and support technicians with a shared goal: to deliver reliable, scalable, and user-centric services that make technology work for people, not against them.

Our Journey

What started as a freelance IT support initiative in 2022 has grown into a full-service tech solutions provider. We began by supporting healthcare facilities with system setup and digital tools.

Today, HYDRATECH continues to serve sectors like healthcare, education, logistics, and retail, building everything from websites to IT infrastructures.

With each project, we've refined our process, learned new technologies, and strengthened our commitment to quality and client success.

Our Mission
